How to play

Defences: tap a defence in the tray, then tap the grass near the path to place it. Tap a placed defence to open its upgrade panel - spend compost to raise its damage and range.

Compost: your currency. You start with some and earn more by defeating pests. Spend it on new defences and upgrades.
