How to play

Defences: tap one in the tray, then tap the grass near the path to build it. Tap a built defence to open its upgrade panel and spend gold on damage/range.

Gold: earn it by killing invaders. Spend it on new defences and upgrades.

Matchups (damage multiplier - every defence has a real weakness, so mix it up):

Win / lose: survive all 8 waves. Let too much damage through and the castle falls.
