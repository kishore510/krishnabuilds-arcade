How to hold the line

  • Tiers 3 & 2 on the wall hold ranged units (archer, gunman, cannon) - tap a glowing circle to place one.
  • Ground slots by the gate hold melee units (spearman, cavalry) - they actively chase down anything that gets close, and cavalry will even sortie out after long-range bombards.
  • Tap an already-placed unit to upgrade or sell it.
  • Enemies that reach the wall (or a bombard firing from range) damage your Castle HP at the top - if it hits 0, you lose.
  • Earn gold per kill. Survive all the waves with the castle standing to win!
